About Lukundo Uniforms

With over 15 years of experience in embroidery and uniform manufacturing, we pride ourselves on quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Our Commitment to Quality

Lukundo Uniforms has been a trusted provider of school uniforms and custom curtains since 2008. We specialize in precision embroidery that lasts, using only the highest quality fabrics and threads.

We partner with schools across the region to provide uniforms that students are proud to wear, and we help homes and institutions create beautiful spaces with our custom curtain solutions.
